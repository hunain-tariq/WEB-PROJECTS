About Us


Welcome to Coffee House!!


At Coffee House, we believe that a great cup of coffee is more than just a drink—it's an experience. Our passion for coffee drives us to source the finest beans, roast them to perfection, and deliver them to your doorstep with care and dedication.


Our Story


Coffee House was born from a love of coffee and a dream to share that love with others. Founded in 2018 by M Hunain Tariq and team, our journey began as a small neighborhood café in the heart of Pakistan. With a commitment to quality and a focus on community, we quickly grew from a local favorite to a beloved coffee brand with a nationwide presence.


Our Mission


Our mission is simple: to provide exceptional coffee experiences that enrich your everyday moments. We strive to bring you the best of the coffee world, from carefully selected beans to expertly crafted blends, all while fostering a sense of community among coffee lovers.


Our Values


  • Quality: We are meticulous in our selection of coffee beans, ensuring that only the highest quality beans make it to your cup.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to ethical sourcing and environmentally friendly practices, supporting sustainable farming and fair trade.
  • Community: We believe in the power of coffee to bring people together, and we are dedicated to building a community of passionate coffee enthusiasts.

Meet out team


  • M Hunain Tariq - Co-Founder & CEO: With a background in culinary arts and a lifelong passion for coffee, Hunain oversees our operations with a keen eye for quality and innovation.
  • Sumaya Zaman - Co-Founder & Head Roaster: Sumaya’s expertise in coffee roasting ensures that every batch is perfectly roasted to highlight the unique flavors of each bean.
  • Fahad-Bin-faiz - Marketing Director: Fahad’s creativity and love for coffee help us share our story and connect with our community.

Our Coffee Philisopy


We believe that great coffee starts with great beans. That’s why we source our beans from trusted farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Our roasting process is both an art and a science, designed to bring out the best in each bean and deliver a consistently excellent cup of coffee.


Join our community


At Coffee House, we’re more than just a coffee brand—we’re a community. Follow us on social media, join our coffee club, and be a part of the Coffee House family. Share your coffee moments with us using #coffeehouse and connect with fellow coffee lovers.


Thank you for being part of our journey. Here’s to many more cups of blissful coffee moments!
